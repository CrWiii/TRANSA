Transportes Nacionales S.A. "TRANSA" is a company with extensive experience, specialized in providing comprehensive solutions in transportation services and road projects in mining and construction.

We have trained and specialized staff to provide an efficient service with quality and safety. We also have a configured fleet according to the type of service required by our customers.

In this way we fulfill our mission and reason for being, which is the satisfaction of our customers in the various economic sectors, meeting their needs and requirements.
 
Providing solutions to our customers, thereby contributing to the progress and development of organizations in the sectors of mining, industry and commerce. To achieve these objectives we manage all transportation by road that our clients require, in addition to the execution of mining and construction projects.
 
To be a major company nationwide, providing solutions to the various economic sectors. In addition, also have the same high level of recognition in the development of engineering and implementation projects in mining and construction.
 
Our quality is manifested through our unwavering commitment to customers to fully satisfy their requirements and expectations, we guarantee to foster a culture of quality in our activities based in our principles and values:
 
1. SPIRIT OF SERVICE: As the main cultural value. The satisfaction of our customers is the goal and motivation of our activities.
2. EXCELLENCE: as A main feature of our work. Being able to achieve objectives and provide solutions to problems demonstrating, in every decision and activity, the high quality of our skills and capabilities.
3. LOYAL COMMITMENT: To our customers and company. Feeling that the problems of our customers and our organization are our problems too, so it is our responsibility to address them.
4. HUMAN DEVELOPMENT: We value and recognize the skills of our employees. We seek their development as people and improve their quality of life.
5. SECURITY: In the activities and decision-making in order to preserve the integrity and welfare of the people. TRANSA considers human resources as a vital part of the productive effort. Therefore we focus on continuous improvement in order to achieve effectiveness at work, and to improve the safety culture within and outside of work as value for survival, growth and development.
6. ENVIRONMENTAL PRESERVATION: Support the protection, minimization and pollution prevention that our activities may produce or generate, allowing more efficient and reduce the risk associated with people and the environment.
7. SOCIAL RESPONSIBILITY: Being supportive to the development and progress of the communities and towns where we travel, respecting their customs and traditions.
8. CREATIVITY AND INNOVATION: As part of our daily challenge for continuous improvement activities, anticipating the needs and desires of our customers and partners.
